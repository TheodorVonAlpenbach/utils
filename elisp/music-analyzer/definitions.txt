Silecnce
	Define

Sound	
	Non-silence time period

Sound source (or just source)
	Entity capable of producing sounds.
	

Simple sound source (or just source)
       Sound source capable of producing only one particular frequency
       (spectre) at a time
       Examples: a piano key, trumpet, human voice

Composite sound source (or just composite source)
       Sound source composed of several simple sound sources. 
       Example: piano, flute, violin, timpani

Sound element (or just sound)
	Non silent time period where all frequencies are produced by
	one sound source entity

Simple sound element (or just sound)
       Sound element produced by a simple sound source

Composite sound element	(or just composite sound)
       Sound element produced by a composite sound source
